Introduction

Mr Tan Chee Hin (born Oct 1980) founded Intech Precision Eng. (M) Sdn. Bhd. on 14 February 2014 in the Batu Maung industrial area with 2 employees. We specialize in “Fabrication Tooling House with High Precision Components” in the engineering field, including CNC Auto-Lathe, CNC Turn-Mill Specialist, CNC Turning, Manual Milling, Jig & Fixtures, Automation Part, and Fabrication Parts.

We achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification under UKAS (U.K) Group in 2019, ensuring consistent customer satisfaction. Our clients come from diverse industries such as Semiconductor, Medical, Aerospace, Automation, and Oil & Gas. We have also diversified into project-based services such as Facilities and Construction, offering one-stop solutions to meet customer needs.

Company Growth Summary

We began in 2014 with 2 machines (1 Turn-Mill, 1 Turning) and 2 employees. Over 9 years, we have grown to 10 machines and 23 employees by 2023. The implementation of ISO 9001:2015 enabled us to expand our business. In 2023, we also acquired Tools Measurement Equipment for CMM.
